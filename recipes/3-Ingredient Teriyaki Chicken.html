3-Ingredient Teriyaki Chicken

Description

This 3-Ingredient Teriyaki Chicken is a total game-changer - with its perfect balance of salty and sweet, this dish is sure to kick up your weeknights with minimal effort required.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cube
  • 1 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar

    • Steps

    1. Heat a large nonstick pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and sear until golden brown on both sides.
    2. Add the soy sauce and brown sugar and stir to combine, then bring to a boil. Cook until the sauce reduces and coats the chicken.
    3. Serve with rice, if desired. Enjoy!
