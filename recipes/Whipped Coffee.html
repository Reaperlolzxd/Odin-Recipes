Whipped Coffee

Description

This viral South Korean drink only requires three ingredients and packs a caffiene-filled punch!

ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of hot water
  • 2 tablespoons of suagr
  • 2 tablespoons of instant coffee powder
  • Milk
  • Ice

    • Steps

    1. Add the hot water, suagr, and instant coffee to a bowl.
    2. Either hand whisk or use an electric mixer until the mixture is fluffy and light.
    3. To serve, spoon a dollop over a cup of milk with ice in it and stir.
